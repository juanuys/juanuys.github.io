+1 News comprises natural language parsing, and news generation by way of Markov chains, aggregation and downstream publishing.

How it works: it crawls the web for linkbait using linkbaiter, then crawl those links, markov the crap out of them, generate new crappy news, then publishes it to Tumblr.
