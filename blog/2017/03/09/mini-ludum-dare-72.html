I participated in another MiniLD.

The code is Clojurescript, the graphics done with Quil (which uses Processing under the hood). The synth sound-effects are raw Web Audio courtesy of hum.

The point? Outlast the bot. Your max paddle hits gets submitted to a Ludum Dare leaderboard - the first of its kind.
