Football Crawl

Fact File
Category game, 7drl
Tech used godot

Behold, my 2021 7DRL entry.

My mate Tim suggested we attend 7DRL. I saw Laser Cup just a few days before, and fancied the idea of a footballer stuck in a dungeon. I liked the idea of incorporating a continuous game mechanic (football) in the usually turn-based Roguelike setting.

Have fun!
